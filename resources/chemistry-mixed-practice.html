How to use this 🚀

These are mixed questions with no single topic — exactly like a screening test. Click an option: green = correct, red = wrong, and a full explanation appears so you learn from every one. Your score tracks bottom-right.

Exam tips for AFIT chemistry:

  • Master the basics first: atomic number = protons, mole = mass ÷ molar mass, OIL RIG (Oxidation Is Loss, Reduction Is Gain of electrons).
  • Learn the general formulas: alkanes CₙH₂ₙ₊₂, alkenes CₙH₂ₙ, alkynes CₙH₂ₙ₋₂.
  • Know your constants: Avogadro = 6.02×10²³, molar gas volume at s.t.p. = 22.4 dm³, pH 7 = neutral.
  • Read every option before choosing — the examiner loves a "nearly right" distractor.
